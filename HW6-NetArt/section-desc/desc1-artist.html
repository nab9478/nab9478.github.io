Jon Rafman, born in 1981, is a Canadian Internet Artist. Rafman is multidisciplinary artist known for his work in video, animation, photography, sculpture and installation. As an internet artist, Rafman explores the dictatorship of the technology from an empathetic lens and is critical of its impact on contemporary. 9 eyes is his most famous work and skyrocketed his career as a renown and recognized artist. Today, Rafman continues to produce more work.
